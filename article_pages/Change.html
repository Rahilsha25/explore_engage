CHANGE

We all witness one or the other change in our life. But what does ‘change’ means.

Change is the difference in what we were yesterday, what we are today and what we’ll be tomorrow. We grow physically and mentally and this growth in us is called the change. When things change inside you, things also change around you and if we don’t change, we don’t grow. Change is the one constant in our lives and it is unavoidable.

Sometimes the past can hold us back, but moving on is very important. Those little changes that occur every day helps to motivate our progress and put us one step further towards our goal. Past is also important as your present and future. When you feel that your growth is slow and you are still miles away from your goals, take a look back at how far you have come from where you have started. Change enables you to become the person you want to be. However, one thing is for sure. Things will not stay the same no matter how much we would like them to.

We all want to give up in some part of our journey and we all come across these types of questions in our life. What if it didn’t work out again, What if I fail again?
The biggest problem with our lives are those ‘What if’s’ that are holding us back. What if your one job is over and the experience you gained from it will help you achieve your next dream. My friend, it’s all about believing in yourself and daring to step forward in your journey. Your failures will make you strong. So don’t sit back, head up and move on and don’t let “What if’s” hold you back.

Too often, we think of "success" or "doing what we love" as an end. It's not the end, it's some destination that we have to reach. What so many people fail to realize is that both "success" and "doing what we love" are just the part of the journey and not the end itself. You are "successful" when you are walking your path, always learning and always growing.

The greatest discovery of all time is that a person can change his future by merely changing his present. The future depends on what step you take today and move on. However, change is an important part of our personal development journey. Accept who you are and move on.

"Change is a continuous process and unavoidable."


- By SHAHILSHA
