PERFECT

What is Perfect?

It's a very common word we use or listen to daily. Right, but what exactly it means?

So before sharing my views, let me first ask you that what does perfect means to you?

I know the answer would be very common from all of us and that is, a thing, situation or any moment which satisfies all our needs, is Perfect.

But do you know, everyday things change, every day our needs also change and so the meaning of perfect changes. Now you all might be thinking that if this is not the meaning then what does perfect means? The meaning of perfect varies from person to person.

For Me,
When I was a kid in school the meaning of perfect was to get good grades and getting appreciated and when I joined college the meaning of perfect changed. Now perfect was to get the best placement and set my career. So as the time changed the meaning of perfect also changed for me.

But then I decided to know what is perfect for me, I started to explore myself and tried to learn more about myself. I realize that things were changing. The way I think, understand, learn, and grow. Now I don't think that getting good grades or the best job is Perfect.

Now the meaning of perfect to me was being different from the rest of the world.

This world is full of competitions and everyone is running after some or the other thing. I just want to be me, do what I like the most and follow my passion.

Ask yourself today that are you following your passion and doing what you like?
Perfect is following your passion and doing what you love. I don't want to be that person who is running every single day on the same track and doing what he/she doesn't even like.

I want my life to be different which means PERFECT.
