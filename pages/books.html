The Begining of Forever

Author - Shahilsha Diwan

At a very early age, my life taught me a lesson that no dictionary or any words could define love. While the sun, the moon, and every star in the universe follow a specific set of rules, love has no rules to follow. It is free.
This Book is a short love story. A story about a Boy and a Girl. The two strangers, how they met and fell in love.
